Decor Ideas

Simple, stylish ideas to help you refresh your home.

Use neutral foundations: Soft colors like beige, ivory, taupe, and gray create a calm and timeless look. They also make it easy to add accent colors later.

Layer textures: Combine wood, fabric, glass, metal, and ceramic elements to make a room feel richer and more interesting.

Focus on lighting: Lamps, natural light, and warm bulbs can completely change the mood of a room.

Style the walls: Art, mirrors, shelves, and simple framed prints can give blank walls a more complete look.

Keep surfaces balanced: Avoid over-decorating. A few carefully chosen objects often look better than too many small items.

Mix old and new: Combining modern and vintage pieces can make your space feel more personal and unique.

Think about flow: Decor should support the way you actually use a space, not just how it looks in a photo.
